For the SparkFun Thing Plus ESP32 WROOM C the ADC can be at the GPIO pins
- A5 GPIO35 ADC1_CH7 
- A4 GPIO36 ADC1_CH0
- A3 GPIO39 ADC1_CH3
- A2 GPIO34 ADC1_CH6
- A1 GPIO25 ADC2_CH8
- A0 GPIO26 ADC2_CH9
- ADC2_CH6 GPIO14 04
- ADC1_CH4 GPIO32 06
- ADC1_CH5 GPIO33 10
- ADC2_CH7 GPIO27 11
- ADC2_CH5 GPIO12 12
- ADC2_CH4 GPIO13 13
